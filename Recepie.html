    How to make the cake

  • Add 200g butter and 150g sugar together
  • Add 2 dinner spoon of lemon juice
  • Add 4 eggs and mix all ingredients very well
  • Take a second bowl and add 250g wheat flour and 1 package of baking powder
  • Grate the peel of the lemon and add 1 tea spoon of lemon abrasion
  • Add all ingredients of the second bowl to the Butter sugar mixture
  • Now mix all ingredients together well
  • Use some butter to grease the cake pan
  • Put the dough in the cake pan
  • Heat the oven to 160 degrees celsius convection
  • Bake the cake for 30 minutes and then cover it with aluminum foil so that it does not get too dark.
  • Continue baking the cake for 15 minutes and after that, cool the cake for 10 minutes

    • How to make the glaze

  • Squeeze the lemon juice from the lemon
  • Add 300g powdered sugar to the lemon juice
  • Mix the ingredients well
  • Pour the glaze over the cake
